Welcome to Ushetu Modern Pre & Primary School

Ushetu Modern Pre & Primary School is located at Nyamilangano ward - which is Ushetu District Council Head Quarter, Kahama District in Shinyanga region. We are a modern English Medium pre, primary day and boarding school.

The school is well equipped with all necessary infrastructures for providing quality education to our pupils at all levels. The school is fully registered by the Ministry of Education, Science and Technology with registration number EM: 18895 for Pre & Primary School and registration number 2968 for Day Care Centre.

We provide child education following the Tanzania syllabus at a relative low cost, with much emphasis on positive character building while providing utmost care to every child enrolled at our school.

Our School Facilities

Ushetu Modern School comprises of three entities: Day-care Unit, Nursery and Primary school. Our facilities include:

  • Adequate and spacious compounds
  • Well-equipped classrooms for effective learning
  • Conducive and well-protected hostels
  • Modern sanitation facilities (toilets)
  • Kitchens, stores, and dining room
  • A variety of playing grounds

Boarding Facilities

Our boarding section has all necessary facilities with full-time electricity and water supply. We provide standard beds to suit the age needs of pupils with supportive care from trained and committed matrons.

Transport Services

Our day scholars have options of either using our school buses transport or using their private transport. For those opting to use the school buses, we provide safe and comfortable transport service. The cost of transport services depends on distance from the school.

Our Passionate Staff

Ushetu Modern School has a well-structured administration, competent, committed and well-experienced teaching professionals, and well-trained supporting staff. We ensure children have a better learning environment and place vital importance on the physical and emotional well-being of our pupils.

We provide the best possible curriculum as guided by the Ministry of Education, Science and Technology with excellent teaching and learning as key priorities. The school fosters a sense of family among pupils and staff.

Food and Nutrition

As a Day and Boarding Pre & Primary school, we provide:

  • Breakfast at 7:00 am for boarders
  • Morning tea at 10:00 am for all pupils
  • A well-balanced delicious meal at 12:30 noon for the whole school
  • Supper for boarders in the evening
  • Safe cool drinking water throughout the day

Note: The school does not allow outside food/bites/drinks to be brought in.

Director's Message

Welcome to Ushetu Modern School, a happy and successful community where every pupil is recognized as having potential and where we aim for all to reach their dreams. We strongly believe that every child has a talent and can experience success as they develop through the supportive and caring environment we provide.

At our School, we respect the traditional values of hard work, good manners, and self-discipline, whilst also preparing our pupils for their future lives in an ever-changing world. We benefit from a dedicated and well-qualified staff who bring 21st century learning alive for everyone.

The best way to fully experience and appreciate what our School has to offer is to visit during a normal school day. I therefore invite you to contact us to arrange a visit or to answer any further questions you may have regarding our school.

I look forward to working with you and your child now and over the coming years.
