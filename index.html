Welcome to Ushetu Modern Pre & Primary School

Ushetu Modern School is a modern English Medium pre, primary day and boarding school located at Nyamilangano ward - which is Ushetu District Council Head Quarter, Kahama District in Shinyanga region.

The school is well equipped with all necessary infrastructures for providing education to our pupils of all present levels. The school is fully registered by the Ministry of Education, Science and Technology.

Quality Education

We provide child education relating to Tanzania syllabus at a relative low cost, with much insistence on positive character building.

Boarding Facilities

Our boarding section has all necessary facilities with full-time electricity and water supply, and standard beds to suit the age needs of pupils.

Transport Services

Our day scholars have options of either using our school buses transport or using their private transport.

Our Core Principles

Our Mission

Ushetu Modern Pre & Primary School provides a positive, vibrant and inspiring environment where pupils are valued and encouraged to succeed in becoming lifelong learners.

Our Vision

Ushetu Modern Pre & Primary School encourages the development of the whole pupils. Our aim is to support learners in becoming curious, reflective and critical in their thinking as patriotic Tanzania and global citizens.
